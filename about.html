about me.


Hi! My name is Rimar Boter, and you can call me 'Rimar' or 'Boter.' I am a freshman student at Mindanao State University - Iligan Institute of Technology under Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. Living with passion is what I strictly adhere to. “Always do what you love” how much ever hackneyed this sounds, it holds absolutely true. That's why I do many things most people around me know of.

I dance, write stories and poems, do music editing and music covers, and write songs too; sometimes, I do graphic designs or video editing, or even explore themes and skins for my laptop and try them out. But my greatest passion is music.

My interest in computers grew when I had my first ICT subject in my junior high school years. It was entertaining doing HTML codes and Turbo Pascal languages, which our teacher taught us the only programming language aside from HTML. Although I somehow lack knowledge about other programming languages, I am willing to learn and know more about them.

I have a lot of subjects that I like, but I have no favorite in particular. However, PE wasn't my thing in High School. My dislikes are bitter foods, a nosy environment, and more things I can't name anymore. But trust me, they're not that harsh. My likes are jammings, fantasy books, exchanging song recommendations, video games, and more.

I am always proud of myself and what I do, especially if it makes me happy. I think that is one thing I want my circle to realize; although I'm an outgoing person, I still have things I keep to myself. Overall, I am fun to be with, and I assure you we'll have a good time together! See you!
