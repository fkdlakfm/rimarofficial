How To Cook Rice Filipino Style? Check out the steps below!

1. First, gather the necessary ingredients, including the rice and the pot.
2. Place the desired quantity of rice in the pot (1 cup is good for one person).
3. Rinse and drain the rice twice or thrice to remove any remaining coating. While rinsing, use your hand like a rake.
4. Add enough water to completely cover the rice; gently jiggle the pot to level it out.
5. The traditional Filipino method of measuring water is to dip the tip of your fingers directly into the pot until they touch the rice and then add more water till it reaches the first knuckle of your middle finger.
6. Cover the pot and place it on a high heat setting (remember to remove your finger before covering it or putting on high heat).
7. Keep an eye on the pot until it is on the verge of boiling over, then lift the lid, count to three, and replace the cover halfway.
8. Reduce to low heat and continue cooking until the water has evaporated to the point where the rice is moist and bubbling but no longer immersed in water.
9. Remove from heat and completely cover. In 5-10 minutes, the rice will be ready to serve.
There you go! You just cooked rice the traditional Pinoy method!
